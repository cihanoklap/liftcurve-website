Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

LiftCurve LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates software tools used internally for mobile user acquisition and creative distribution. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our tools and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including account credentials and configuration data necessary to connect third-party advertising platforms (such as Meta, Google, AppLovin, and Unity Ads). We also collect file metadata from connected cloud storage services (Google Drive, Dropbox) solely to facilitate creative uploads.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively to operate our internal tools — uploading creative assets to advertising platforms and managing campaign configurations on behalf of authorized users. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except as required to provide the service (e.g., passing creatives to ad platforms via their APIs).

3. Meta Platform Data

Our application connects to the Meta Marketing API to upload ad creatives and manage campaigns. We access only the permissions necessary for these functions. Meta user data accessed through our app is not stored beyond what is required for the current session and is not used for any purpose other than fulfilling the requested upload or campaign action.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and credentials for connected platforms are encrypted at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Data Retention

We retain job and upload records for operational purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using our data deletion page.

6. Third-Party Services

Our tools integrate with third-party platforms (Meta, Google, AppLovin, Unity Ads, Dropbox). Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@liftcurve.com.
