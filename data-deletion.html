Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 6, 2026

If you have connected your Meta account to LiftCurve's tools and would like your data removed, follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites.
  2. Find Creative-Uploader in the list and click Remove.
  3. This revokes our app's access to your Meta account immediately.
  4. To request deletion of any data we may have stored, email us at info@liftcurve.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm by email once complete.

What Gets Deleted

Upon request, we will delete any stored access tokens, account identifiers, and upload history associated with your Meta account from our systems.
